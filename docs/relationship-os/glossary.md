# RelationshipOS Glossary

## Relationship

A person the user intentionally wants to remember, understand, or tend. "Relationship" is preferred over "contact".

## Weekly Care

The weekly ritual that surfaces three to five people with a reason, hook, last touchpoint, and suggested next care.

## Tended

A private weekly completion marker. Marking someone tended removes them from the current week's suggestions without creating a streak or score.

## Hook

A human reason to reach out, such as a new role, hiring signal, move, shared article, or collaboration need.

## Next Care

A gentle next step for a relationship. It is not a dated task in MVP.

## Import Inbox

Holding area for imported people. Imported rows do not become relationships until the user keeps them separate or confirms a merge.

## Source Fingerprint

SHA-256 import dedupe key stored as operational metadata. It prevents repeated Google imports from creating duplicate inbox rows while raw Google identifiers remain encrypted.

## Relationship Dimensions

Private 1-5 reflective signals: closeness, energy, and opportunity.

## Lifecycle Stage

Current relationship state: Discover, Acquaintance, Active, Trusted, Dormant, or Archived.

## Value Ledger

Contextual memory of value given or received. It is not a scorecard.

## Connection

A known relationship between two people in the user's private graph. MVP stores connections but does not show a visual network map.
