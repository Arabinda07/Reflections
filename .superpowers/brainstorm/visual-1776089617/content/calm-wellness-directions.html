Which Calm Companion Direction Feels Right?

All three keep the product gentle and non-clinical. The difference is what the app leads with after someone journals.

A. Journal-First Sanctuary

The writing space is the star. AI and stats sit nearby as gentle side panels after the user writes.

B. Guided Check-In Companion

The app opens with a daily check-in, mood, intention, and supportive next step before the deeper note.

C. Insights-First Wellness Center

The dashboard leads with trends, streaks, and patterns, with journaling as the main action underneath.

My early recommendation is A with a little of B: make writing feel safe and spacious, then use AI as a gentle companion after the user has expressed something.
